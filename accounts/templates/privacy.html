Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how aayohai (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application for online room booking, event ticketing, and grocery shopping.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, billing/shipping address.

  • Booking & Order Data: Details about your room bookings, event tickets, and grocery orders.
  • Device Info: Device type, OS version, IP address, and usage patterns (used for analytics and security).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve app functionality and services.
  • To process orders, payments, and bookings.
  • To communicate order status, confirmations, and customer support.
  • To enhance security and prevent fraud.
  • To analyze usage for improving user experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Your data may be shared with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe) for secure transactions.
  • Service providers (e.g., delivery/logistics partners, SMS/email services).

4. Data Security

We implement strict technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information. Payments are encrypted and handled securely via Stripe’s infrastructure.

5. Your Rights

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
